Investigation aids
- Check Set-Cookie: SameSite, Secure, HttpOnly
- Check if session cookie lacks SameSite (defaults to Lax)
- Check responses for Access-Control-Allow-Origin/Headers (for CORS context)
- Confirm presence/absence of Origin and Referer on target requests

Meta referrer directives for PoCs
<meta name="referrer" content="no-referrer">
<meta name="referrer" content="unsafe-url">

CRLF cookie seed (URL-encoded) example
%0d%0aSet-Cookie:%20csrfKey=YOUR-KEY%3b%20SameSite=None
